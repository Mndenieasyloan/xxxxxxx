Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about V0 Trade

How do I create an account?

▼

Click the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and fill in your email, password, and other required information. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.

Is V0 Trade secure?

▼

Yes! We use bank-level security with 256-bit encryption, cold storage for 95% of funds, and 24/7 monitoring. We also offer $500M insurance coverage.

What are the trading fees?

▼

Our trading fees start at just 0.1% per trade. Volume traders can qualify for even lower fees through our tier system.

How long do withdrawals take?

▼

Crypto withdrawals are processed within 30 minutes. Bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days depending on your location and bank.

What is Bitcoin mining?

▼

Bitcoin mining is the process of validating transactions and adding them to the blockchain. Our cloud mining service allows you to earn Bitcoin without owning expensive hardware.

Can I cancel my mining plan?

▼

Mining plans are contract-based. You can cancel at any time, but you'll forfeit the remaining contract period. Contact support for more details.

How do I enable 2FA?

▼

Go to your Account Settings → Security → Enable Two-Factor Authentication. Download an authenticator app like Google Authenticator and scan the QR code.

What cryptocurrencies can I trade?

▼

We support 150+ cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Cardano, and many more. Check our Cryptocurrencies page for the full list.
